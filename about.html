About Me

Hi there! I’m Alex and I’m a graphic designer. I have been practicing design for five years and I’ve worked professionally as a designer for four years. I became a designer because I decided to rebel against my parents, like every teen does, and I enrolled in a design class in high school. From there I fell in love and never turned back. If you get to know me, you’ll see that I have two rescued cats named Wasabi and Sake and I will never stop talking about them. In my free time I enjoy playing video games with my friends and playing with my cats. Check out my social medias below to see more of my work!
