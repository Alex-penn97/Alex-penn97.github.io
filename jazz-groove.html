Freelance: Jazz Groove

Jazz Groove was a freelance project I worked on in the fall of 2018. One of my coworkers who was a developer approached me with the project for help with the front end development. We had about two months to finish the website. The clients gave us the high fidelity mockup of the first page only and gave us freedom to make the rest of the website reflect that design.
