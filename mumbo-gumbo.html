All That Mumbo Gumbo

All That Mumbo Gumbo is a project from the fall of my senior year. We were tasked with the creation of our own food truck concept, menu, to go items, and the truck itself. I chose to do a gumbo “truck” because I grew up eating creole and cajun food since my mother grew up in New Orleans and it’s my go to comfort food. The “truck” is a street car found in New Orleans that has been converted into a food serving vehicle. Half of the street car is the kitchen and the other half is a dining section since gumbo is traditionally meant to be eaten in a social setting. The colors and overall design style represents the culture and styles of New Orleans. I kept my menu small and simple and I tried to cater to a general population by giving vegans and vegetarians an option, people with shellfish allergies and option, and classic gumbo lovers and option.
