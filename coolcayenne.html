Cool Cayenne: Screen Printing Designs

Cool Cayenne is a small silk screen printing business located in Muncie, Indiana. As the graphic designer, I created various works for schools, churches, businesses, etc. I also handled the project management side of the business, allowing me to get a clear understanding of what the client needed for their shirt design and build a relationship with the client. Screen printing has many limitations, one being usage of many colors, so I had to be conscious of the printing process and the budget of the client in order to create an eye catching design.
