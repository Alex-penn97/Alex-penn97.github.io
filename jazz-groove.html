Freelance: Jazz Groove

Jazz Groove was a freelance project I worked on in the Fall of 2018. One of my coworkers, who was on the development team, approached me with the project. He needed my assistance with the front-end development of the website because he had little knowledge of CSS. The clients gave us the high-fidelity mockup of the first page only and gave us freedom to make the rest of the website reflect that design.

The home page you see below represents the only design that they gave us to go off of. Each page after the home page did not have a design, so I created my own small style guide and used that to ensure all pages looked and felt cohesive.
